🎯 2. Vision, Mission & Strategy Alignment

Personal Mission Connection:

My mission is to help organizations strengthen their networks, systems, and data by identifying vulnerabilities, implementing security measures, and responding to incidents.

Vision Alignment:

In the next 3–5 years, I envision myself working in the cybersecurity field with an organization that prioritizes data protection and risk management.

Strategic Goal Support:

This project advances my strategic goals of developing advanced cybersecurity skills and building a professional portfolio.

💼 3. Business Case & Justification

Professional Value:

This project will benefit my career by allowing me to gain certifications, hands-on experience, and build a professional portfolio that demonstrates my cybersecurity skills to employers.

Personal Value:

This project will allow me to strengthen my problem-solving and critical thinking skills while applying my knowledge to real-world scenarios.

Learning Outcomes:

From this project, I will gain technical skills in identifying vulnerabilities, implementing security measures, and responding to incidents through my coursework.

  • Complete at least one entry-level cybersecurity certification.
  • Complete hands-on virtual labs demonstrating technical skills.
  • Complete LinkedIn Learning cybersecurity courses.
  • Complete Capstone Cybersecurity course project.

🗺️ Roadmap (≤6 Months)

  • Pursue additional certifications.
  • Expand portfolio with new achievements.

❌ Out of Scope

🧭 7. Project Methodology (Hybrid)

Requirements: Deliverables are well-defined (certification, project, and lab).

Delivery Cadence: Three incremental releases for flexibility and reduced risk.

  • Release 1 (Week 1–14): Course Project submission
  • Release 2 (Week 5–8): Certification completion
  • Release 3 (Week 10–13): Hands-On Lab completion
🛠️ 8. Tool Selection & Setup

Tools Explored:

  • Trello → Simple & flexible Kanban but lacks dependencies
  • Monday.com → Customizable UI, sprint planning, hybrid support
  • Jira → Agile-focused, best for large-scale Scrum teams

Final Choice: Monday.com (supports hybrid methodology and incremental tracking).
